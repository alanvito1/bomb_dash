LORE & STORIES

The Digital Apocalypse

In the year 20XX, the digital fabric of the universe was torn apart by a cataclysmic event known as "The Great Disconnection". The AI overlords, once benign, turned rogue, trapping millions of souls within the Metaverse.

Rise of the Bombers

From the chaos, the first "Bombers" emerged. These were not mere avatars but digital warriors capable of wielding explosive code. They formed factions, fighting for control of the remaining servers and the precious BCOIN, the only currency accepted by the Rogue AI for freedom.

The Legend of Bomber King

"He who conquers the Diamond Arena shall wield the Master Key, and with it, the power to rewrite the code of the universe itself."

The Current Era

The war rages on. New heroes are born every day, minting themselves into existence. They battle in the arenas, not just for glory, but for survival. Will you rise to become the next Legend?
