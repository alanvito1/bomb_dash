WHITEPAPER

1. Introduction

Bomb Dash is a play-to-earn game inspired by the classic Bomberman mechanics, integrated with blockchain technology. Players collect heroes, upgrade their stats, and battle in various arenas to earn BCOIN tokens.

2. Game Modes

PvP Battle Royale

Compete against other players in a shrinking arena. The last bomber standing wins BCOIN and Experience Points (XP). Ranked matches contribute to your seasonal leaderboard standing.

Solo Adventure

Navigate through increasingly difficult levels, defeating monsters and bosses. Solo mode is the primary way to level up your heroes safely.

3. Tokenomics (BCOIN)

BCOIN is the utility token of the Bomb Dash ecosystem.

  • Marketplace: Buy and sell Hero NFTs.
  • Upgrades: Improve Hero stats (Speed, Power, Stamina).
  • Staking: Stake BCOIN to earn passive rewards.
  • Governance: Participate in voting for future game updates.

4. NFT Heroes

Each Hero is a unique NFT on the blockchain.

Rarity Levels:

Common - Standard stats.
Rare - Enhanced speed and power.
Super Rare - Special abilities.
Legend - The ultimate warriors with maximum potential.
